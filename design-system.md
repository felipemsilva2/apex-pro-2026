# Apex Pro Design System (Landing Page)

## Typography
- **Headings**: Syne (Extra Bold / Black), Italic, Tracking Tighter.
- **Body**: Plus Jakarta Sans / Inter.

## Colors
- **Primary**: #D4FF00 (Bolt Lime)
- **Secondary**: #1A1A1C (Dark Onyx)
- **Background**: #0A0A0B (Absolute Black)
- **Accents**: White/10 for glass effects, White/5 for borders.

## UI Patterns
- **Neobrutalism**: Sharp edges, high contrast, -skew-x-12 for buttons and accents.
- **Glassmorphism**: Backdrop blur with subtle white borders.
- **Animations**: Fade-in with slight vertical slide.

## Layout
- Wide containers, generous padding-vertical (py-24 or py-32).
